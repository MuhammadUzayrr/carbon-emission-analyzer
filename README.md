# Carbon Emission Analyzer

## About

Carbon Emission Analyzer is a community-level carbon footprint tracking system that collects, processes, and visualizes greenhouse gas emission data from multiple sources within a locality. It was built as part of the Transparency International Youth Climate Fellowship and presented at an international SDG conference.

The system allows communities, organizations, or researchers to log emission data across categories such as transportation, energy consumption, and industrial activity, then aggregates that data into meaningful visual reports. The goal is to make environmental impact measurable and actionable at a grassroots level rather than relying solely on national or corporate reporting.

## Why It Is Helpful

Most carbon tracking tools are designed for enterprises or governments. Individuals and local communities have almost no practical way to understand their collective footprint or compare changes over time. This project fills that gap by offering a lightweight, accessible platform where community members can contribute data and see the real-world impact of their collective behavior.

It supports SDG Goal 13 (Climate Action) by turning abstract environmental statistics into local, tangible numbers. It also enables advocacy: when communities can quantify their emissions, they gain evidence to push for policy change, infrastructure improvements, or behavioral programs.

## What Should Be Added

- User authentication and role-based access so community coordinators can manage data entry teams
- Integration with public datasets such as national grid emission factors or satellite land-use data to supplement manual entries
- Predictive modeling using historical trends to forecast future emissions under different behavioral scenarios
- A mobile-friendly data entry interface so field volunteers can log readings on-site
- PDF and CSV export of reports for use in formal submissions or grant applications
- Multi-community comparison dashboards to benchmark one locality against similar ones
- Notification system that alerts users when emissions cross a set threshold
- Support for carbon offset logging so communities can also track reduction efforts alongside emissions
